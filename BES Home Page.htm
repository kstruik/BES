Lawn BOWLS EVENT SCORING (BES)

You have reached the landing page for Lawn Bowls Event Scoring.

BES is a general-purpose EXCEL based event scoring workbook for scoring lawn bowls events and tournaments.

The workbook is based on a series of workbooks attributed to Geoff Graham but is under constant development.  Please check back here for the most recent version.
